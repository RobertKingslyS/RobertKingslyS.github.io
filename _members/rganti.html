--- layout : member name : Radha Krishna Ganti designations : - Assistant Professor email : rganti@ee.iitm.ac.in areas : - Wireless Networks - Communication Systems. image : rganti.png homepage: http://www.ee.iitm.ac.in/~rganti/ bio : Radha Krishna Ganti  is an Assistant Professor at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai, India. He was a Postdoctoral researcher in the Wireless Networking and Communications Group at UT Austin from 2009-11. He received his B. Tech. and M. Tech. in EE from the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, and a Masters in Applied Mathematics and a Ph.D. in EE from the University of Notre Dame in 2009. His doctoral work focused on the spatial analysis of interference networks using tools from stochastic geometry. He is a co-author of the monograph Interference in Large Wireless Networks (NOW Publishers, 2008). He received the 2014 IEEE Stephen O. Rice Prize,  and the 2014 IEEE Leonard G. Abraham Prize and the 2015  IEEE Communications society young author best paper award. type: faculty ---
