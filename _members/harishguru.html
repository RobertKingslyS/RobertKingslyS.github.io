He received his B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from College of Engineering Guindy, Anna University, and his ME in Systems Science & Automation from IISc. His research interests include the interface of statistics, learning theory, and convex analysis, and currently center on statistical consistency properties of algorithms for multiclass learning and structured prediction tasks.
