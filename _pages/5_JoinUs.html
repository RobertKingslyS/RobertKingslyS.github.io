Open Positions

Post-doctoral fellows

Please see here for details. Women candidates have special fellowships from IIT Madras. Send us your CV and a short write-up of your proposed area of research at PostDoc@rbcdsai.org.

Post Baccalaureate Fellowships

RBCDSAI invite applications from highly motivated, recent B.E/B.Tech graduates with outstanding academic record, passion for research for One year Post Baccalaureate Research Fellowships. Eligible candidates please apply with C.V, Github profile, SoP, details of blogs to PostBac@rbcdsai.org.

Project Assistants/Associates

We expect applicants to have strong programming background in either Python or R. We also encourage applicants with programming experience in relational platforms such as SQL and the following non-relational technologies: Hadoop, Map Reduce, Hive, Mahout. Experience in PIG and information retrieval libraries like Lucene or Solr is an added advantage.

Additionally, applications are invited for a Junior Research Fellow (JRF) position in a project funded by the Department of Biotechnology at the Computational Systems Biology Lab. BE/BTech/MTech in any branch of engineering, with a GPA of 7.5 and above. Programming experience in at least one high-level programming language (e.g. Python) is mandatory. Some exposure to biological data analysis will be helpful; Familiarity with NGS data analysis pipelines / RNASeq will be a major plus.

Interested candidates can send their CV to associate@rbcdsai.org.

PhD/MS

Applications must be made on-line, through IIT Madras website.
Applications are also welcome under the Interdisciplinary Research Programme (IDRP) at IIT Madras. There will be a special stream, "Data Sciences & AI" for applying to RBC-DSAI. Applicants must be fluent in at least one high-level programming language (C/C++/MATLAB/Python). A background in mathematics is highly preferred.

Internships

There are multiple positions available at present. Please write to us at internship@rbcdsai.org with your detailed CV and your Statement of Purpose.
